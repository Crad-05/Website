Ríomhaire OS

Ríomhaire is an open source Linux-based operating system that is designed to prioritize user privacy and give users control over their hardware. It offers user-friendly interfaces, making it a great choice for those who are new to computers or those who are changing OS from Windows 10 or 11. It can be installed on a variety of 64-bit devices.
